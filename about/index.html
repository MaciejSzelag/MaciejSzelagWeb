About me

Frontend & Backend Developer Custom Website Creator

Hi, I’m Maciek – a web developer with a tech mindset, a human touch, and a solid dose of life experience. I design and build fast, modern, and user-focused websites and web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and Laravel. I value clean code, usability, and results – no unnecessary noise, just meaningful work.

I don’t work on autopilot. I take the time to understand your goals, offer practical solutions, and bring the right tools to the table. Whether it’s a simple website or a complex application, I believe every project deserves attention, clarity, and care.

Outside of development

My path to tech wasn’t typical, but that’s what makes it powerful. I bring both maturity and a fresh, modern approach to my work. Outside of development, I’m a husband, father of three, and a Brazilian jiu-jitsu instructor at Checkmat Plymouth. BJJ teaches me discipline, focus, and humility – values I carry into every collaboration.

And yes, tech runs in the family: my oldest son is beginning a PhD in cybersecurity, specializing in cryptography and artificial intelligence.
